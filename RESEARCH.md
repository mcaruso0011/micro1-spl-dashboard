# micro1 Research — Context for Building

Quick reference on micro1's business, products, and the SPL role. Use this as context when working with Claude Code.

---

## Company Overview

**micro1** is the end-to-end human data infrastructure behind AGI. They recruit, vet, and deploy domain experts (PhDs, professors) to generate high-quality training data for frontier AI labs and Fortune 10 enterprises.

- **Founded**: ~2022 by Ali Ansari (while at UC Berkeley)
- **Valuation**: $500M (Series A, Sept 2025)
- **Revenue**: $100M+ ARR (as of Dec 2025, up from $7M at start of 2025)
- **Funding**: $35M Series A led by 01 Advisors
- **Team**: Remote-first, managing thousands of experts across 100+ domains and 60+ languages
- **Clients**: Leading AI labs (including Microsoft), Fortune 100 companies

## Core Products

### Zara — AI Recruiter
- Interviews and vets candidates in 20+ languages
- Auto-scores applicants for expert roles
- Accepts top 1% of applicants (Stanford profs, Harvard PhDs, senior engineers)
- Trained on thousands of human interviews (zara-1.3 model)

### Data Engine — Human Data Platform
- Converts expert intelligence into structured training datasets
- Manages execution at scale with performance tracking
- Measures velocity, error rates, cost per task, quality in real-time
- Layered review: experts → managers → human data leads → AI QC

### Astra — Performance Management
- Dashboarding system that quantifies expert quality, velocity, reliability
- Creates performance signals for bonuses and replacements
- Feeds back into vetting and QA systems

### Rhea — AI Quality Control
- AI-based QC trained to detect structural errors in expert-generated data
- Learns from project instructions
- First layer of multi-model QC across environments

### Realm — RL Environments & Evaluations
- Expert-level human data for training frontier models
- RL environments and frontier evaluations
- Domain-specific capability improvements

### Ava — Proctoring Model
- Trained on 30,000 cheating probability data points
- Ensures integrity of expert work

## Growth Areas

1. **Enterprise AI Agents** — Non-AI-native Fortune 1000s building internal agents need evaluation, fine-tuning, and validation
2. **Robotics Pre-Training** — Building world's largest robotics dataset via real-world demonstrations (shipping Ray-Ban glasses kits)
3. **RL Environments** — Virtual workspaces for training AI agents on simulated tasks

## The SPL Role

**Strategic Projects Lead** — Main POC for top AI lab customers.

### Core Responsibilities
- Manage teams of PhDs/professors training frontier AI models
- Ensure highest standards in data integrity, scalability, quality
- Oversee design and execution of workflows for large-scale data pipelines
- Act as the primary client interface

### What They're Looking For
- 2+ years leadership driving operational processes
- Experience managing large-scale data projects or optimizing workflows
- Passion for building from the ground up
- Identifying inefficiencies and executing solutions rapidly

### Compensation
- $140K–$200K base (varies by location/seniority)
- Equity compensation for all employees
- Performance-based bonuses
- Health insurance reimbursement up to 100%
- Remote-first

micro1 differentiates through **expert quality** (top 1% acceptance) and **white-glove service** (managed execution with layered QA).

## Key Insight for the Demo

The dashboard should reflect what makes micro1 different: **quality over volume**. The metrics that matter most are expert accuracy, data integrity, and the multi-layered review process — not just raw throughput. An SPL at micro1 isn't just a project manager; they're the quality guardian for frontier model training.
